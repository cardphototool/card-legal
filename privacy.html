Privacy Policy

Effective date: 2026-04-30

Important: This app does not upload, collect, back up, or share the images you import. Image processing is performed locally on your device, and the app does not upload your original or processed images to developer servers.

1. Card Image Processing

This app is designed for capturing, importing, correcting, cropping, laying out, saving, and exporting card-type images.

The app does not automatically upload your images to the cloud or to developer servers. The app does not perform face recognition, does not actively extract document numbers, and does not create user identity profiles.

2. Information the App Processes Locally

To provide its core functions, the app accesses or processes the following information locally on your device:

  • Images that you actively select or capture;
  • Corrected images or PDF files generated and saved locally by the app;
  • Purchase, subscription, or lifetime license status returned by Google Play.

3. Permissions

  • Camera permission: used to take photos of card-type images.
  • Photos/Storage permission: used to read images you choose and save corrected images or PDF files to your device.
  • Network permission: used for Google Play purchases, subscription/license status verification, and opening the Terms of Service and Privacy Policy pages.

The app does not request contacts, location, microphone, SMS, or other permissions unrelated to its core functions.

4. Use of Information

The app uses relevant information only to:

  • Provide image correction, cropping, layout, saving, and export functions;
  • Verify Google Play purchases, subscriptions, or lifetime license status;

The app does not use the images you import for advertising, profiling, model training, or any purpose unrelated to the app’s core functions.

5. Sharing of Information

We do not sell, rent, or share the images you import or your personal private information.

Google Play payments, subscriptions, cancellations, and refunds are processed by Google Play services. The developer does not receive your bank card number, payment account, or full payment credentials.

6. Data Storage and Deletion

Images and PDF files that you import, capture, correct, or export are stored locally on your device. You can delete them at any time from your gallery or file manager.

After uninstalling the app, the app will no longer access your device information or local files. Google Play purchase records are managed by your Google account and Google Play services.

7. Updates to This Policy

If this Privacy Policy is updated due to feature changes, legal requirements, or Google Play policy requirements, we will provide notice in the app or on this page for material changes. Continued use of the app after the changes become effective means you acknowledge and accept the updated policy.

8. Contact

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or data handling, please contact us using the email address at the bottom of this page.
