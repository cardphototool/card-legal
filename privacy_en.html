Privacy Policy

Effective date: 2026-04-30

“ID Card Photo Correction & Layout Tool” is developed and operated by Plues Kam. This Privacy Policy explains how this app accesses, uses, stores, and protects user information.

Important: This app does not upload, collect, back up, or share your ID card photos, driver license photos, bank card photos, passport photos, green card photos, insurance card photos, or similar sensitive images. Image processing is performed locally on your device.

1. Image and ID Information Processing

This app is designed for capturing, importing, correcting, cropping, laying out, saving, and exporting ID card or card images.

The app does not automatically upload your images to the cloud or to developer servers. The app does not perform face recognition, does not extract ID numbers, and does not create user identity profiles.

2. Information the App May Process

To provide its core functions, the app may access or process the following information locally on your device:

  • Images that you actively select or capture;
  • Corrected images or PDF files generated and saved locally by the app;
  • Purchase, subscription, or lifetime license status returned by Google Play.

If crash reporting or diagnostic SDKs are added in the future, the app may collect non-sensitive diagnostic information such as app version, device model, system version, and crash logs only for bug fixing and app improvement. Such changes will be reflected in this policy and disclosed in accordance with Google Play requirements.

3. Permissions

  • Camera permission: used to take photos of IDs or cards.
  • Photos/Storage permission: used to read images you choose and save corrected images or PDF files to your device.
  • Network permission: used for Google Play purchases, subscription/license status verification, and opening the Terms of Service and Privacy Policy pages.

The app does not request contacts, location, microphone, SMS, or other permissions unrelated to its core functions.

4. Use of Information

The app uses relevant information only to:

  • Provide image correction, cropping, layout, saving, and export functions;
  • Verify Google Play purchases, subscriptions, or lifetime license status;
  • Fix issues and improve the app experience.

The app does not use your ID images for advertising, profiling, model training, or any purpose unrelated to the app’s core functions.

5. Sharing of Information

We do not sell, rent, or share your ID images or personal private information.

Google Play payments, subscriptions, cancellations, and refunds are processed by Google Play services. The developer does not receive your bank card number, payment account, or full payment credentials.

6. Data Storage and Deletion

Images and PDF files that you import, capture, correct, or export are stored locally on your device. You may delete them at any time from your gallery, file manager, or within the app where applicable.

After uninstalling the app, the app will no longer access your device information or local files. Google Play purchase records are managed by your Google account and Google Play services.

7. Children’s Privacy

This app is not directed to children under the age of 13 and does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

8. Updates to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy due to feature changes, legal requirements, or Google Play policy requirements. For material changes, we will provide notice in the app or on this page. Continued use of the app after the changes become effective means you acknowledge and accept the updated policy.
